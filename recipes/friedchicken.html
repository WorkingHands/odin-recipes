Description

This recipe will generate the most crispy and flavorful fried chicken that you have ever tasted in your lifetime.
It will not require you to waste any oil because we are using an airfryer for this recipe.
Only the basic seasonings are required but you can use your favorite seasoning to match your taste.

Ingredients

  • Two pounds of chicken thighs/drumsticks
  • Ziplock bag
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Paprika
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Wash your choice of chicken
  2. Dry pat the chicken with paper towels then set aside.
  3. Put a quarter cup on baking powder into the ziplock bag.
  4. Add the listed seasonings into the ziplock bag at your taste.
  5. Close the ziplock bag and shake to mix the seasonings.
  6. Add the chicken into the bag and shake again to cover the chicken.
  7. Shake off excess mixture from the chicken then place them into the air fryer(Caution not to place them on top of each other).
  8. Set the airfryer to Airfry, Temperature to 390, and Time to 31 minutes. Flip chicken every 10 minutes.
  9. Voila!! Cool the chicken then serve with whatever choice of carbs you would like. (My favorite is sticky rice).
